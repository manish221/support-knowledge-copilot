# Product FAQ

## Password reset
Users can reset their password from the Sign In page by selecting **Forgot password**. A reset link is sent to the user's verified email address. The link expires after 30 minutes.

## MFA reset
Users who still have access to their primary email can reset MFA from **Account Settings > Security > Reset MFA**. If the user does not have access to email, they must contact Support for identity verification.

## Billing contact changes
Account administrators can update billing contacts from **Admin Console > Billing > Contacts**. Standard users cannot update billing contacts.

## Browser support
The portal supports the latest two stable versions of Chrome, Edge, Firefox, and Safari. Internet Explorer is not supported.
