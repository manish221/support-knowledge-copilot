Support priority levels
P1 incidents are production outages affecting all users or critical business workflows. P1 cases receive a target first response within 30 minutes.

P2 incidents are major functional issues affecting a subset of users. P2 cases receive a target first response within 4 business hours.

P3 incidents are general support requests or minor issues. P3 cases receive a target first response within 1 business day.

Escalation
Customers can request escalation when a P1 or P2 issue remains unresolved beyond the target response window. Escalation requests must include business impact and affected tenant ID.
